Hi, I’m Lisa! I’m currently pursuing my Master’s degree in Management and Analytics at New York University, specializing in Business Analytics. I earned my Bachelor’s degree from Carnegie Mellon University in Computational and Applied Mathematics, with a minor in Computer Science, which gave me a strong foundation in quantitative analysis and technical problem-solving.

This summer, I’m interning as a Data Engineer at Capital Rx. My work focuses on developing robust data pipelines and models to analyze internal ticket data. Leveraging tools such as dbt, MWAA Airflow, and Snowflake, I’m helping the company build scalable systems that support performance tracking and strategic decision-making at the leadership level.

I’m deeply passionate about working with data, whether it’s designing architectures, writing clean and efficient code, or translating raw data into actionable insights. I love bridging the gap between technical solutions and real business outcomes.

I’m now seeking full-time opportunities in data engineering, analytics engineering, data analytics, and data science.
